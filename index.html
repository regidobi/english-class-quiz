Welcome, Player!
Before you start the quiz, let's review some important English Tenses.
Present Simple: Used for daily routines and facts.
Example: I study English.

Present Continuous: Actions happening right now.
Example: I am studying English now.

Past Simple: Completed actions in the past.
Example: I studied English yesterday.

Future Simple: Actions that will happen.
Example: I will study English tomorrow.
